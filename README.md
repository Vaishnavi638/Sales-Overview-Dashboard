# Sales Overview - Excel Dashboard

Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales Overview & Insights Dashboard developed using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance across regions, product categories, sales representatives, payment methods, and customer segments.

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights using Excel’s advanced analytics and visualization features.

🎯 Objectives

Analyze overall sales and profit performance

Compare regional sales distribution

Evaluate product category performance

Monitor monthly sales trends

Understand customer behavior (New vs Returning)

Assess the impact of discounts on sales

Track sales representative performance

📊 Dashboard Features

🔹 Interactive Filters (Slicers)

The dashboard includes dynamic slicers for:

Region (East, North, South, West)

Product Category (Clothing, Electronics, Food, Furniture)

Sales Representative

Months (Jan–Dec)

Users can interactively filter data and instantly view updated insights across all visualizations.

📈 Key Visualizations

1️⃣ Sales by Region

Bar chart comparing regional sales performance for different years.

2️⃣ Sales Over Month

Line chart showing monthly sales trends to identify seasonality and performance fluctuations.

3️⃣ Total Profit by Category

Bar chart analyzing profitability across product categories.

4️⃣ Product Sales Distribution

Donut chart representing contribution of each product category to total sales.

5️⃣ Sales by Payment Method

Comparison of revenue generated through:

Bank Transfer

Cash

Credit Card

6️⃣ Impact of Discount

Line chart analyzing how discounts influence sales volume.

7️⃣ Customer Count (New vs Returning)

Horizontal bar chart showing customer segmentation by region.

8️⃣ Sales by Representative

Performance comparison of individual sales representatives.

📌 Key Insights

Certain regions consistently outperform others in total revenue.

Furniture and Clothing categories contribute significantly to total profit.

Credit Card and Bank Transfer are major revenue-generating payment methods.

Higher discounts show noticeable impact on sales fluctuations.

Returning customers form a strong portion of the customer base in multiple regions.

Sales performance varies significantly among representatives, indicating areas for performance optimization.
